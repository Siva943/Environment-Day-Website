Medicinal & Air Purifying Plant

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is one of the world's most valuable medicinal plants, widely recognized for its healing, skincare, and environmental benefits. Its easy maintenance and numerous uses make it an excellent addition to homes, gardens, and green campuses.

🌿 Scientific Name

Aloe barbadensis Miller

🌍 Family

Asphodelaceae

☀ Sunlight

Bright Direct Sunlight

💧 Watering

Once Every 1-2 Weeks

About Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera has been used for thousands of years in traditional medicine. The gel found inside its leaves is rich in vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and amino acids that help nourish and protect the body.

It is commonly used in skincare products, herbal remedies, and wellness treatments. Aloe Vera also improves indoor air quality and requires very little maintenance, making it ideal for sustainable gardening.

🌱 Health Benefits

  • Supports skin health
  • Promotes wound healing
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Natural moisturizing properties

🌿 Environmental Benefits

  • Improves air quality
  • Absorbs indoor pollutants
  • Supports eco-friendly gardening
  • Requires less water

🪴 Care Guide

  • Use well-drained soil
  • Avoid overwatering
  • Provide adequate sunlight
  • Protect from extreme frost

Interesting Facts About Aloe Vera

300+

Species of Aloe plants exist worldwide.

5000 Years

90% Water
